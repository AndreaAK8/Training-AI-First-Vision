# WIN 6: STREAMLINED TRAINING FEEDBACK SURVEY
## Universal Survey for All Roles (8 Questions - 3-4 Minutes)

### **Form Configuration**
**Form Title:** "Training Program Feedback - Help Us Improve!"  
**Description:** Your feedback matters! This short survey (3-4 minutes) helps us continuously improve our training programs. Your responses will be analyzed using AI to identify patterns and enhancement opportunities.

---

## **CORE SURVEY QUESTIONS** 
*Designed for maximum insights with minimal effort*

### **Q1. Overall Training Satisfaction**
How satisfied are you with your overall training experience?
- **Scale:** 5-Excellent, 4-Good, 3-Satisfactory, 2-Needs Improvement, 1-Poor
- **Required:** Yes

### **Q2. Role Readiness**
How confident do you feel performing your role after completing training?
- **Scale:** 5-Very Confident, 4-Confident, 3-Somewhat Confident, 2-Need More Support, 1-Not Ready
- **Required:** Yes

### **Q3. Training Content Quality**
Rate the relevance and quality of the training content for your role.
- **Scale:** 5-Excellent, 4-Good, 3-Satisfactory, 2-Needs Improvement, 1-Poor
- **Required:** Yes

### **Q4. Coach & Trainer Support**
How would you rate the support provided by your trainer and coach?
- **Scale:** 5-Excellent, 4-Good, 3-Satisfactory, 2-Needs Improvement, 1-Poor
- **Required:** Yes

### **Q5. Training Pace**
Was the training pace appropriate for your learning?
- **Options:** Too Fast / Just Right / Too Slow
- **Required:** Yes

### **Q6. What Worked Best?**
What was the most valuable part of your training experience?
- **Text Area** (Required - minimum 20 characters)
- **AI Focus:** Success pattern identification

### **Q7. What Needs Improvement?**
What aspect of the training needs the most improvement?
- **Text Area** (Required - minimum 20 characters)
- **AI Focus:** Problem identification and improvement recommendations

### **Q8. Additional Feedback**
Any other comments or suggestions to help us improve?
- **Text Area** (Optional)
- **AI Focus:** Comprehensive sentiment and theme analysis
